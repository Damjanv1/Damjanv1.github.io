## Dashboards

Interactive, data-led dashboards published as standalone pages.

### [Professional Dashboard CV](https://damjanv1.github.io/cv-dashboard/Damjan-Vlastelica-Dashboard-CV.html)
My professional CV presented as an interactive dashboard &mdash; experience, skills, capability footprint, and career KPIs, rendered with Chart.js.

### [Australian Federal Budget 2026&ndash;27](https://damjanv1.github.io/cv-dashboard/australian-budget-2026-27.html)
An Economist-style infographic covering the 2026&ndash;27 Federal Budget &mdash; revenue vs expenditure, deficit trajectory, key policy measures, and a scatter chart of surplus/deficit across all budget years with estimate badges and the current year highlighted.

### [Chess Performance Dashboard](https://damjanv1.github.io/cv-dashboard/chess-dashboard.html)
A data-led look at how I've been playing on Lichess since August 2023 &mdash; rating trajectory, opening repertoire, material dynamics, phase analysis, and more. Refreshed weekly from the Lichess API via GitHub Actions. Filter by time control (All / Rapid / Blitz).

### [NSW Fuel Insights Dashboard](https://damjanv1.github.io/cv-dashboard/fuel-dashboard.html)
A near-real-time view of NSW petrol prices &mdash; postcode + radius search, fuel-type filter, drive-cost calculator, cheapest / most-expensive suburbs, brand and LGA breakdowns, and a hybrid map (heatmap &rarr; LGA choropleth &rarr; station markers as you zoom). Refreshed hourly from the NSW FuelCheck API via GitHub Actions.

## Analysis & Reports

### [The Valuation of Ambition: SpaceX S-1 Analysis](https://damjanv1.github.io/spacex-s1-analysis.html)
A Harvard Business Review&ndash;style teardown of SpaceX&rsquo;s $1.75 trillion IPO filing &mdash; word frequency analysis across all 280 pages, TAM vs revenue butterfly charts by segment (log scale), circular investment web, Fortune 500 revenue rank comparison, valuation multiples vs Google / Amazon / Tesla / WeWork, and a competitive landscape review.
