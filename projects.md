## Dashboards

Interactive, data-led dashboards published as standalone pages.

### [Professional Dashboard CV](https://damjanv1.github.io/cv-dashboard/Damjan-Vlastelica-Dashboard-CV.html)
Find out about my work experience to date and how to get in touch with me. Given that I work with dashboards and visualisations I used this project as a way to rethink the CV and showcase both my work and my experience. The intention of this &lsquo;Dashboard&rsquo; CV is to tell you not only about my experience but to give you an indication of what I am like to work with and what I believe in the data analytics space. It also serves as a visual demonstration of my communication and presentation abilities. Built using Chart.js.

### [Australian Federal Budget 2026&ndash;27](https://damjanv1.github.io/cv-dashboard/australian-budget-2026-27.html)
An Economist-style infographic covering the 2026&ndash;27 Australian Federal Budget &mdash; revenue vs expenditure, deficit trajectory, key policy measures, and a scatter chart of surplus/deficit across all budget years with estimate badges and the current year highlighted.

### [Chess Performance Dashboard](https://damjanv1.github.io/cv-dashboard/chess-dashboard.html)
For those of you that know me, mid-life crisis is chess (<a href="https://damjanv1.github.io/2021/03/08/chess.html">see more here</a>). As a youngster ranked in the top 100 of my state, I was for a time even top 20. However as I got older I played less and less before taking a near 20 year hiatus &mdash; before a WhatsApp group of dads at my daughter&rsquo;s school rekindled my interest in the game. Now I play a lot, and I decided to use the opportunity to create an analytics tool that tracks my progress, playing style, and identify weaknesses (both my own and that of my opponents). Refreshed weekly from the Lichess API via GitHub Actions. Filter by time control (All / Rapid / Blitz).

### [NSW Fuel Insights Dashboard](https://damjanv1.github.io/cv-dashboard/fuel-dashboard.html)
Feel like fuel is getting more expensive? More importantly, would you like to know whether driving to a further station for cheaper fuel is worth the cost? Unsatisfied with current apps on the market that purport to solve this problem? This dashboard gives a near-real-time view of NSW petrol prices &mdash; postcode + radius search, fuel-type filter, drive-cost calculator, cheapest / most-expensive suburbs, brand and LGA breakdowns, and a hybrid map (heatmap &rarr; LGA choropleth &rarr; station markers as you zoom). Refreshed hourly from the NSW FuelCheck API via GitHub Actions.

## Analysis & Reports

Long-form analytical pieces with embedded data visualisations.

### [The Valuation of Ambition: SpaceX S-1 Analysis](https://damjanv1.github.io/spacex-s1-analysis.html)
A Harvard Business Review&ndash;style teardown of SpaceX&rsquo;s $1.75 trillion IPO filing &mdash; word frequency analysis across all 280 pages, TAM vs revenue butterfly charts by segment (log scale), circular investment web, Fortune 500 revenue rank comparison, valuation multiples vs Google / Amazon / Tesla / WeWork, and a competitive landscape review. Data sourced directly from the S-1 PDF.

### [Australian Occupations &amp; AI Exposure](https://damjanv1.github.io/au-ai-jobs.html)
A canvas treemap of ~14 million Australian workers across the ANZSCO occupation classification &mdash; sized by employment, coloured by AI exposure. Tracks how exposure estimates evolved across four published studies from 2021 to 2026 (Felten, Eloundou, Gmyrek/ILO, and a Karpathy-style LLM prompt). Toggle between AI Exposure, Job Growth, Median Pay, and Education colour modes. Data: ABS Labour Force, Jobs &amp; Skills Australia projections, and crosswalked AI exposure indices.
