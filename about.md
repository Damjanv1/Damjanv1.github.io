I'm Damjan Vlastelica — a data and analytics leader based in Sydney, Australia. I currently lead reporting, analytics, and AI adoption at [The Iconic](https://www.theiconic.com.au), Australia's largest fashion e-commerce platform. Before that: PwC, Qantas (Insurance and CTO), ACMA, Mirvac, and a handful of others across financial services, consulting, property, and government — around two decades of turning data into decisions across most of the industries that matter in Australia.

The through-line across all of it is the same: most organisations have more data than they know what to do with, and the gap between the data they have and the decisions they're actually making is where the interesting work lives. My background sits at the intersection of analytics, strategy, and the communication of complexity — building things that make the invisible visible, and making sure the people who need to act on insight can actually read it.

## What this site is

A working portfolio and a place to think out loud. Most of what's here is built for a reason — I found a question I wanted to answer, or I wanted to demonstrate an approach, or I got frustrated with an existing tool and decided to build a better one.

The projects range from the practical (a real-time fuel price dashboard that tells you whether the drive to the cheap station is actually worth it) to the analytical (a treemap of Australia's 14 million workers tracking how AI exposure has shifted across four years of research) to the personal (a chess performance analyser that helps me understand why I keep losing to the Caro-Kann).

The format is usually the same: a single self-contained page, built to be used rather than just looked at, with the data and methodology visible rather than hidden. If you work in data, you'll probably recognise the thinking. If you don't, the goal is that it should be readable anyway.
